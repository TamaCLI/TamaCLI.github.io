🤔 What in the World is TamaCLI?

Remember Tamagotchi? Well, we thought, "Why should phones have all the fun?" So we created TamaCLI - a delightful little pet that lives right in your terminal! It's like having a tiny, digital friend who hangs out while you code.

No more lonely debugging sessions or silent deployments. Your terminal buddy is there to cheer you on with commands like tamacli feed (because even virtual pets get hungry during those long coding sessions).

✨ Features That'll Make You Go "Aww!"

  • Lives in your terminal (no pet deposit required!)
  • Gets hungry while you code (just like a real developer)
  • Shows emotions based on your command success rate (it feels your pain)
  • Needs care and attention (but won't wake you up at 3 AM)
