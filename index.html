TamaCLI 🐣

Terminal got lonely? Not anymore! 😼

🐾 What is TamaCLI?

TamaCLI is a playful, terminal-based pet inspired by Tamagotchi. It lives inside your prompt, gets hungry over time, and needs your care with commands like tamacli feed.

💻 Demo

✨ Features

  • Lives inside your terminal
  • Tracks mood, hunger, and health
  • Feed and heal your pet
  • More fun commands coming soon!
